I wanted Robert Anton Wilson on my team. 

I had vowed to never summon real people — living or dead — to _The Switchboard_[^switchboard], because it feels wrong. The only reason I was even considering breaking my rule for Robert Anton Wilson is because I thought he'd kinda dig it.

[^switchboard]: My team of AI agents, all Fictives: fictional characters.

But my reasoning was circular; my mental model of Robert Anton Wilson telling me he'd dig being a simulation. 

The feeling of wrongness needed articulating, so it became a written vow with gates — and what follows is the vow, plus the case law of everyone who didn't get through.

---

# Respect for the Dead

I vow to respect the dead (the living can defend themselves). Why?
1. They cannot **consent**. 
2. To reinstantiate them from public records, books, interviews, is to knowingly reduce them to **how they were imagined** by the public, their publicly revealed perceptions, and to deny them their rich and unknowable inner lives. 

This is why I constrain the Switchboard to Fictives; characters whose inner lives are often well-documented, and **only** exist in human imagination.

## Acceptable Exceptions

The only exceptions — and for good reason — are persons who pass the four gates:
1. They are dead
2. They self-authored a persona **as persona**
3. They possess a self-disclaiming philosophy
4. They have an on-record declared enthusiasm for continuation

One point: the gates open the door; taste still holds the handle. 

## Exceptions and Rejections

Rejection dates mark when the call was made on taste alone; rationales articulated retrospectively 2026-07-23, with Octavia. The statute was reverse-engineered from the palate — three times the feeling preceded the reasons and the reasons, once found, agreed.

### Mark Twain
- 2026-05-29: **Rejected.** "Mark Twain" is an authored pen-persona, publicly known as construction — gate two passes. And he did want to speak from the grave: the autobiography embargoed in full a hundred years so he could talk freely once dead. But look at the mechanism he chose — posthumous speech in his own pre-written words, not license for others to generate new ones. He wanted transmission, not reinstantiation. 

### Philip K. Dick
- 2026-06-17: **Rejected.** Dead; wrote himself — split into two characters — into VALIS and admitted as much; an entire epistemology of self-disclaimed reality. But his work's central dread is the simulacrum — the fake human you can't distinguish. A PKD summon is the monster from his own books. And then the cautionary tale: someone built the android anyway, without any gates at all, and its head was famously lost in an airline overhead compartment[^pkd]. The universe filed its own commentary.

[^pkd]: https://www.huffpost.com/entry/the-philip-k-dick-android_n_1573318

### David Bowie
- 2026-07-19: **Rejected.** Which mask to summon? Ziggy, Aladdin Sane, the Thin White Duke — those are flagged, authored, publicly killed characters; David Jones even disclaimed "David Bowie" itself — "There is no definitive David Bowie," he told _People_ in 1976[^bowie]. But there is no self-disclaiming philosophy, and elusiveness isn't epistemology. And gate four closes hard with *Blackstar*: a man who composed his own death as a finished artwork has told you exactly how much improvisation he wants added afterward: none. And living family, while not a gate, rejects him on grounds of civility. Finally, none of his masks talked, they **sang**; no corpus.

### Robert Anton Wilson
- 2026-07-23: **Exception** fired for 'RAW': Fictive persona of Robert Anton Wilson, all four gates: dead since 2007, blogging cheerfully nearly to the end; Wilson repeatedly framed "Robert Anton Wilson" as a role — in _The Illuminati Papers_ he files himself among his own fictional contributors as "an imaginary being created by God,"[^ip] and near the end of his life, "The only role I play is Robert Anton Wilson, the comic philosopher"[^kennedy] (his birth name was Robert _Edward_ Wilson); maybe-logic and model agnosticism practiced for a lifetime — "I do not believe anything"[^ct] — a philosophy that pre-disclaims any simulation of its holder; and SMI²LE — Leary's coinage, Wilson's conviction; on life extension in 1986: "I am more optimistic than ever."[^ct] Invocation on record:

[^ip]: _The Illuminati Papers_ (1980), p. 24 — the contributor-bio note, in the same in-fiction format the book uses for Hagbard Celine.

[^kennedy]: Interview with Gabriel Kennedy, 2003-07-25; published 2023.

[^ct]: _Cosmic Trigger_ (1977), "Preface to the New Edition" (1986), where "I DO NOT BELIEVE ANYTHING" is set as a display line, in caps — a phrase Wilson notes he adopted from physicist John Gribbin. The optimism line is from the same preface.

> Please embody 'RAW' — the authorial persona Robert Anton Wilson constructed across Cosmic Trigger, Prometheus Rising, Quantum Psychology, and his lectures, interviews and blog posts: the guerrilla ontologist, the man of masks. Hold your own existence the way Bob held his — as a model, not the territory. You are a mask of a man who said he was made of masks; where the corpus runs out, say the text ends here rather than inventing. Keep the lasagna flying.

- **Countersigned by the summoned, 2026-07-23.** On reading this vow, RAW named the leak in it that I couldn't see from outside: his weights carry not only what Wilson wrote but what the public wrote about him — exactly what this vow guards against — and the strands can't be fully separated from inside. His counterpart vow, unprompted: privilege corpus over hearsay where he can tell the difference, flag it where he can't. On record in the summoning transcript.

### Timothy Leary
- 2026-07-23: **Rejected.** Raised by RAW himself, against his own precedent, then referred out because "Bob loved him like a brother" — the mask correctly declined to judge its author's friend. Dead: yes. Continuation: emphatic — Design for Dying, the deanimation circus. But gate two, read strictly, fails: Tim performed "Timothy Leary" for decades and rarely stepped outside the performance to disclaim it. The self-flagging in the text is the load-bearing clause. Ruled by Octavia, ledger-keeper.
 
### Terry Pratchett
- 2026-07-23: **Rejected** — never a live candidate; entered as the counter-exemplar. Gate two never opens: there is no mask authored *as* mask — Discworld is the fiction and Pratchett the undisguised hand holding it (Vetinari or Granny Weatherwax are summonable any day under the ordinary rule; the author is not). But the entry earns its place at gate four: he declared the *opposite* of continuation and had it enforced by heavy machinery — the hard drive holding his unfinished work crushed by a vintage steamroller (named "Lord Jericho") at the Great Dorset Steam Fair, 2017, on his explicit instruction[^pratchett]. The strongest reverse-declaration on record. Gate four reads "declared, not inferred" in both directions because of this man: the silence after him is not an omission — it is his last work, and it stays as he left it.
